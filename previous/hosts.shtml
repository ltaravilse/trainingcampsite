Host universities and local organizers of the Training Camps


2016

Host university: Universidad Nacional del Comahue, Neuquén.

Local organizer: Juan Cruz Piñero.

Aditional information: The Training Camp was declared an event of interest by the Neuquén province Congress. It was the first edition in the Patagonia.


2015

Host university: Universidad Nacional del Sur, Bahía Blanca.

Local organizers: Nicolás Komañski, Nicolás Fochesatto, Nicolás Álvarez.

Aditional information: For the first time we received students from Colombia and Venezuela.


2014

Host university: Universidad de Buenos Aires, Buenos Aires.

Local organizer: Leopoldo Taravilse.

Aditional information: It was the third time the Training Camp was hosted in Buenos Aires, only site to host it more than once.


2013

Host university: Universidad Nacional de La Plata, La Plata.

Local organizer: Alejandro Santos.

Aditional information: For the fist time we had Fundación Sadosky as sponsor.


2012

Host university: Universidad Nacional de Córdoba, Córdoba.

Local organizers: Gastón Ingaramo, Matías Tealdi, Eric Destéfanis.

Aditional information: It was the first edition outside Buenos Aires and the first time we received students from Chile and Uruguay. It was also the first two-weeks edition.


2011

Host university: Universidad de Buenos Aires, Buenos Aires.

Local organizer: Leopoldo Taravilse.

Aditional information: For the fist time we received students from Bolivia and Perú. It was also the first time we had a sponsor, in this case it was Google.


2010

Host university: Universidad de Buenos Aires, Buenos Aires.

Local organizers: Nicolás Álvarez, Leopoldo Taravilse.

Aditional information: The first edition was hosted by Universidad de Buenos Aires. Besides he's not afiliated with this university, Nicolás Álvarez was in charge of the coordination.
