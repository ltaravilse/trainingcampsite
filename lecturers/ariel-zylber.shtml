Ariel Zylber: Currently pursuing both a M.S. in Computer Science and a M.S. in Mathematics at Facultad de Ciencias Exactas y Naturales, Universidad de Buenos Aires. Gold Medal at the 52° International Mathematical Olympiad (Amsterdam 2011), two times Gold Medal at the Iberoamerican Mathematical Olympiad (2010,2011), Gold Medal at the Mathematical Olympiad of the Southern Cone (2010), Bronze Medal at the 22° International Olympiad in Informatics (Waterloo 2010).
