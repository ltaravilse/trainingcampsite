Melanie Sclar: Currently pursuing both a M.S. in Computer Science and a M.S. in Mathematics at Facultad de Ciencias Exactas y Naturales, Universidad de Buenos Aires. Silver Medal at the 21° Mathematical Olympiad of the Southern Cone (Brasil, 2010); Bronze Medal at the 26° Iberoamerican Mathematical Olympiad (Costa Rica, 2011); National Champion of the 27° Argentine Mathematical Olympiad (2011).
