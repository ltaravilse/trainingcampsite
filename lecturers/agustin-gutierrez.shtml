Agustín Santiago Gutiérrez: M.S. in Computer Science from Universidad de Buenos Aires. Finalist and Latin American Champion twice as contestant (2009 and 2011) and finalist six times as coach (2010, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016 and 2017), being Latin American Champion in 2015. Agustín is a First class Teaching Assistant at the Computer Science Department of Universidad de Buenos Aires - FCEN. He participated twice of the International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI) obtaining a Silver Medal in 2007. He is a jury and coach of the Argentine National Olympiad in Informatics (OIA).
